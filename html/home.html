ABOUT US

Venus Handicraft was established in 1988 as a company specializing in providing ceramic collections for everyone in the world.

Our main focus is on the commercial sector, providing innovative and beautiful products for retail, online, and retail locations.

As you continue through our website, we hope you will see interesting collections of delicate porcelain tiles, romantic natural limestone and innovative glass mosaics that will inspire design. for your next project.

Our ultimate goal is the opportunity to become a valuable resource for architectural and design professionals, as well as their customers.
